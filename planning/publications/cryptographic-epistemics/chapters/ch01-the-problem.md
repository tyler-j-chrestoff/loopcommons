# Chapter 1: The Problem

Why does AI hallucinate? Not "how do we fix it" — why is it structural? The current alignment paradigm treats hallucination as a training data problem or a guardrails problem. Argue that it's an architectural problem: inference without grounding. Reference Coppola (arxiv 2603.17063) if useful but don't assume the reader knows it. End with what a solution would need to look like, without revealing the full framework.
