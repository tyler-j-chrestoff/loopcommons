# Chapter 9: Evolved Identity

What if an agent's identity document isn't authored — it evolves? The biological analogy: you don't write your child's personality. You provide genome, environment, and selection pressure. The three-layer stack: DNA (computational substrate facts, evolves slowest), SOUL (values and identity, evolves per generation), tools (capabilities, evolves per generation). Map to Clune's Hyperagents (2026): meta-meta, meta, task. The author's role shifts from architect to gardener. Identity = lineage, not snapshot.
