# Chapter 6: The Grounding Certificate

Merkle trees as the mechanism for enforcing a finite, grounded concept space. The distinction: verification is infrastructure, not epistemology. The Bayes net does the thinking. The hash tree does the grounding. Every inference node gets a hash. Every posterior has a provenance chain to observation. Hallucination = an inference that can't produce a valid Merkle proof. Connect to content-addressed storage (Git, IPFS) as existing implementations of the same principle.
